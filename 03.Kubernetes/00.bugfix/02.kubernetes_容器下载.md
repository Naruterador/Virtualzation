## 在执行ctr export时报错问题
```shell
#在执行export时报错
root@pacdownload:~# ctr -n k8s.io i export coredns-amd64.tar registry.k8s.io/coredns/coredns:v1.13.1

#出现如下报错：
ctr: failed to get reader: content digest sha256:0f8b424aa0b96c1c388a5fd4d90735604459256336853082afb61733438872b5: not found

#解决方案
#在pull时添加--all-platforms参数就能解决这个问题
ctr -n k8s.io images pull --all-platforms registry.k8s.io/coredns/coredns:v1.13.1



```
